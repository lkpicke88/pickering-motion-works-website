About

Built by someone who understands both sides of the table.

Many production companies understand the creative process. Many institutions understand disciplined execution. Pickering Motion Works knows both — and that changes everything about how a project gets built, approved, and delivered.

Founded by Kevin Pickering, Pickering Motion Works was built on the belief that exceptional creative work deserves exceptional execution. Drawing on decades of experience in federal acquisition and program support, and a lifelong career in professional photography, the company combines artistic craftsmanship with the discipline, accountability, and precision required to deliver complex projects with confidence.

That philosophy shapes every engagement. From independent films and documentaries to commercial campaigns, institutional media, streaming productions, and music projects, clients benefit from a company that understands both creative storytelling and the importance of planning, communication, quality control, and delivering every project to the highest professional standard.
